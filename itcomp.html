99X Technology

History

99X Technology was founded in 2004 in Colombo, Sri Lanka. The company was established by a group of visionary entrepreneurs with the goal of creating a software engineering company that could provide world-class software solutions.

Services And Expertise

99X Technology offers a wide range of services, including software product engineering, custom software development, quality assurance and testing, DevOps, cloud solutions, and more. They are known for their expertise in cutting-edge technologies such as artificial intelligence (AI), machine learning, blockchain, and Internet of Things (IoT). The company has a strong focus on user experience (UX) and user interface (UI) design, ensuring that the software they develop is both functional and user-friendly.

  • Full Stack Development (JavaScript, Serverless)
  • Blockchain (Private & Public Blockchains, Smart Contracts)
  • Cloud Enablement (Microservices, APIs, NoSQL)
  • Mobile Applications (Hybrid, Native, PWA)
  • Machine Learning (Machine Learning, IoT, Big Data)
  • User Experience (Lean UX, Prototyping)
  • Product Conceptualization (Lean Startup, Market Research)
